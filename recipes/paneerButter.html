Recipe of Yummmy Butter paneer

The Recipe of Butter paneer

Butter paneer masala is a popular North Indian dish that features soft, homemade paneer (cottage cheese) cooked in a rich, creamy tomato-based gravy. The combination of flavors is truly delightful.

Ingredients

  • 1500 grams paneer, cubed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 can (400 grams) diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 teaspoon garam masala
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Steps

  1. Prepare the paneer: If you're not using store-bought paneer, you can make it at home by curdling milk with lemon juice or vinegar.
  2. Sauté the onions and spices: Heat butter in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle for a few seconds. Then, add chopped onions, garlic, and ginger. Sauté until the onions turn golden brown.
  3. Add the tomatoes: Add the diced tomatoes to the pan and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the tomatoes break down and the mixture becomes thick.
  4. Add the paneer: Gently add the cubed paneer to the pan and coat it in the gravy. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the paneer is heated through.
