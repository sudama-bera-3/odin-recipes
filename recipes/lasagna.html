The Recipe of lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that has gained worldwide popularity. Steps

  1. Prepare the meat sauce: the ground beef or pork in a large skillet. Add chopped onions, garlic, and herbs. Stir in tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, and spices. Simmer for 30-45 minutes.
  2. Make the cheese sauce: In a separate saucepan, melt butter. Whisk in flour and cook for 1 minute. Gradually whisk in milk until smooth. Bring to a simmer, stirring constantly. Remove from heat and stir in Parmesan cheese and nutmeg.
  3. Bake: Cover the lasagna with foil and bake for 30-40 minutes. Uncover and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until bubbly and golden brown.
  4. it rest: Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it rest for 10-15 minutes before serving. This allows the flavors to meld and the lasagna to set.
